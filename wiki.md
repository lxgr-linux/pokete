<h2 id="effects">Effects</h2>
<p>Those effects can be given to a Pokete through an attack.</p>
<h3 id="paralyzation">Paralyzation</h3>
<p>Paralyses the enemy and stops it from attacking. This is reverted randomly.</p>
<h3 id="sleep">Sleep</h3>
<p>Makes the enemy fall asleep and stops it from attacking. This is reverted randomly.</p>
<h3 id="burning">Burning</h3>
<p>Sets the enemy on fire and damages the enemy with 2 HP every round. This is reverted randomly.</p>
<h3 id="poison">Poison</h3>
<p>Poisons the enemy and damages the enemy with 1 HP every round. This is reverted randomly.</p>
<h3 id="confusion-1">Confusion</h3>
<p>Makes the enemy hurt it self. This is reverted randomly.</p>
<h3 id="freezing">Freezing</h3>
<p>Freezes the enemy and stops it from attacking. This is reverted randomly.</p>
